Questival - a New Race

Questival is a 24-hour adventure race that offers participants points for completing food, fitness, service, teamwork and other wacky challenges. This years questival is Star Wars themed. In the end, only one team will be the senate. Only one will hoist their lightsabers as champions, and be the only ones powerful enough to weild the questival Force!!!
